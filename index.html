Managed Network Infrastructure Lab — Router, Switching & Segmentation

Infrastructure modernization lab focused on moving from a consumer network design to a managed router/switch environment with cleaner topology, network segmentation planning, and standardized infrastructure documentation.

My original home network had limitations around control, segmentation, visibility, and documentation. I wanted a more enterprise-style lab that better reflected managed routing, switching, and network operations.

Troubleshooting

Built a stronger foundation for VLAN segmentation, managed switching, network documentation, and future enterprise-style routing/switching practice.
